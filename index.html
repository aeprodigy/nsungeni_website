Nsungeni Children's Home

Nsungeni Children’s Home is a non-Governmental Child Welfare Organization whose objectives are to provide long and short-term solutions to the children in need of child protection facilities regardless of gender, colour and religious affiliation. Nsungeni is a local name meaning, “KEEP ME”, thus the name Nsungeni Children’s Home. Nsungeni Children’s Home provides Education Support, accommodation, meals, clothing and access to health care to children The facility accommodates a maximum of 22 children by providing shelter, nutrition and access to education and health facilities.

What we want for our children

Our Mission statement

Our mission is to come to the rescue of the orphans
and vulnerable children from social isolation and has access
to all their basic needs, including but not limited to, education,
health, shelter, food and clothing.

Our Vision

Our vision is to facilitate a society where children
are given equal opportunities to live a sustainable
and peaceful life that affirm their self-worth and dignity.

Our Core Values

Social justice and Equity

We will work with other stakeholders to promote equality,
dignity and more just society, especially with regard to the poor and
marginalized.

Gender and Diversity

Our work will reflect and promote respect for gender and
diversity issues.

Accountability

We are accountable and responsible to our actions and
uphold ethical behavior and responsibility.
